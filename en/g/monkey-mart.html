Monkey Mart

TinyDobbins4.63,081,519 votes

Monkey Mart is an idle/management game where you control a cute monkey character who is in charge of a supermarket. Plant fruits, harvest produce, move around from station to station to fill the stands with various food items. Sell bananas, corn, eggs, peanuts, coffee beans, chocolate, wheat etc. Your customers will pick them up and wait for you at the cashier desk - simply stand next to the cash register to collect your money. As you unlock new aisles and grow your market with new products, you can hire assistants to help oversee the maintenance of the aisles and other employees. You can also buy appliances that can prepare advanced products with your harvest such as chocolate bars, coffee, yoghurt, popcorn, peanut butter, muffins, cookies, ice cream, and more! Make sure to upgrade your character's managing skills, unlock new work stations, and teach your staff new skills. Don't forget to wear cool hats and manage your market in style! Do you have the skill it takes to make your farmer's market the best one in town?

How to play Monkey Mart

Run around the market to grab fresh crops and stand next to aisles stack them up for the customers to pick up. You don't need to press any buttons - your character will do all the work as long as you're standing in the right place!

Move - WASD or Arrow keys

What is the latest mart?

Monkey Mart has been updated with the third mart. Now you can produce and sell chocolate muffins, cookies, and ice cream! 🎉

How can I unlock a new mart in Monkey Mart?

Pay attention to the spots the camera focuses on. You must save enough money to build an aisle on that spot. Make sure you've unlocked every mandatory stalls to be eligible to open your next branch.

How switch between marts in Monkey Mart?

Tap the truck icon at the top of your screen to select and move between the branches you own. Alternatively, walk outside the shop and stand next to your truck.

Can I customize my character in Monkey Mart?

Tap the hat icon at the top of your screen to browse the shop and check all the cool hats you can wear.

How can I earn more money in Monkey Mart?

Upgrading your mart is the best way to earn more money and unlock new things faster. Tap the icon with an arrow at the top of your screen to reveal the Upgrades menu. Here you can improve many things like:

  • Player: Helps you carry more items at once so you can be more effective when stacking products.

  • Assistants, Farmers, Animals, and Chef: Helps them be more productive, faster, and more energetic.

  • Appliances: Increases the amount of products the machines can make, and makes them more resistant to breaking down.

How can I collect money from customers?

Simply stand next to the cash register where you'll see cash piling up. Your monkey will automatically collect the money.

What does the Chef do in Monkey Mart?

The Chef is responsible for processing your harvest and making delicious snacks and pastries.

Why are my employees falling asleep in Monkey Mart?

The monkey assistants are working hard so they get tired quickly. You can increase their overall motivation and energy in the Upgrades menu.

What kinds of appliances are there in Monkey Mart?

There are various machines to add some variety to the products you sell such as Microwave, Yoghurt Maker, Grinder, Peanut Butter Maker, Chocolate Maker, Wheat Mill, Ice Cream Machine, Butter Machine, Muffin Maker, and Chocolate Bar Maker.
